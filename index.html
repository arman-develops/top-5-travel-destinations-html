Top 5 travel destinations in Kenya

These are Amon's top 5 kenyan destination picks. It has a blend of the wild, coastal beauty, landscapes and culture

Maasai Mara

This scenery is endowed with big cats and famous for the great migration.

I'd say its the closest thing to magic I've ever experienced

Activities:

  • Occasional visits to the Maa communities
  • Game drives
  • Bird watching
  • Hot air baloon safari

Amboseli National Park

Gotta admit it dear, it has one of the best undefeated views of Mt. Kilimanjaro

The snow toppings get me every single time

Activities:

  • Cultural experiences
  • Safari drives
  • Mountain viewing

Diani Beach

Never had any coral reefs experiences? This is your one single chance.

Honestly, it feels like a real cosplay of the Moana animation, clear blue waters, creamy sands, sea shells. I love it

Activities:

  • Night life
  • Coastal history tour
  • Snorkelling
  • Dhow cruises

Lake Nakuru

I remember describing this scenery as "...looking down you could have easily confused the flamingos to a large pink carpet over the lake".

To date it still feels right to do so amidst all the flamingos

Activities:

  • Picture friendly lake views
  • Picnics
  • Bird watching
  • Wild life drives

Lamu Island

It was described to us by our guide as an Old Town, well, it does speak for itself.

It has preserved its swahili culture, centuries old buildings and structures and the charming people.

Activities:

  • UNESCO World Heritage site
  • Sailing
  • Exploring the Old Town
  • Visiting swahili communities
